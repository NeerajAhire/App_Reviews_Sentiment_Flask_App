About

1. This Webapp provides sentiment analysis of reviews of apps in Google Play Store.

2. Enter App name in the input box and click generate result.

3. It usually takes about half a minute to generate the results.

4. In case of any error, please reload and try again.

5. It will display the following plots-

   Sentiment Pie Chart, Ratings Pie Chart, Overall Words by Frequency, Words by Frequency in Positive reviews, Words by Frequency in Negative reviews
